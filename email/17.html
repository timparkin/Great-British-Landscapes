What's in this issue then?



In this issue we have a new interview video from Paul Gallagher where he talks about his approaches to black and white photography and much more. Max Rush presents a project on Brockwell Park, London through the seasons and Mike Green looks at the human form in landscape photography.

This issue also sees another film test, an epic undertaking that lets us see just how different films render different subject matter. Richard Childs returns with another Letter from Scotland about his ongoing work on the isle of Eigg and Tim Smalley talks about getting into and out of a creative rut.

Our featured photographer this month is Paul Moon - someone who has really made his local patch, the Yorkshire Wolds, his speciality. Finally, David Tolcher reviews the new Rolls Royce of geared tripod heads, the Arca D4.

Hope you enjoy things and don't forget to let us know if you are interested in writing for Great British Landscapes and/or if there is anything in particular you would like us to write about.

What's all this subscription stuff about?

Well, even though the majorty of the content is free, the magazine is still a business venture and it costs a few hundred pounds to run and takes about six or seven full days per issue to create a deploy. Hopefully you'll appreciate the content and want to subscribe and if you do, you'll also get access to the video content that Tim, Joe and our guests produce and also some of the best articles that are written. You can subscribe by clicking on the 'register' link at the top right of the magazine or by clicking on the links at the bottom of every page. Don't forget that your subscription is ongoing (i.e. you will get billed again until you cancel) but cancellation is simple and instructions are included in your subscription emails.

We'll be running the magazine for at least the next 16 months and hopefully it will be a self supporting venture at that point (possibly. Hopefully, earlier!). Thank you for your time and if you already subscribe then thank you for your support.
